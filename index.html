Richard Sankey, Client Investments Director, CCLA Investment Management

Richard is responsible for managing relationships with CCLA’s clients. He joined CCLA in 2022 after 20 years in the investment management industry, advising and managing charity and private client portfolios. Transitioning from an army career, Richard completed an MBA at City University Business School (now Bayes Business School), subsequently working for Hermes Investment Management, Kleinwort Benson and Newton. Richard is a trustee of the Forces Employment Charity (originally The Officers’ Association) and previously sat on the investment committee of the Drapers’ Livery Company and is a governor of Bancroft’s School. He is a Chartered Fellow of the CISI and holds the PCIAM qualification.
